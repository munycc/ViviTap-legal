1. Introduction

ViviTap is a communication assistance application designed for people with communication disabilities and their caregivers ("helpers"). It serves users of all ages, including minors who use the app under parental or guardian supervision. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal data in compliance with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR / DSGVO) and applicable German law.

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Account Data

  • Email address (Firebase Authentication)
  • Username (chosen at registration)
  • Display name (optional)
  • Profile photo (optional, stored in Firebase Storage)
  • User role: helpee or helper

2.2 Communication Data

  • Messages sent and received between connected users (last 50 messages per contact, stored in Firestore)
  • Custom communication buttons (text, emoji, optional image)
  • Button usage statistics (frequency of use per button)

2.3 Technical Data

  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) push token for notifications
  • App preferences: language, grid layout, dark mode, audio volume, send method
  • Connection list (usernames of connected caregivers/users)
  • Crash reports and anonymized diagnostic data (Firebase Crashlytics)

2.4 Call Data (coming in a future version)

  • Audio and video call functionality is planned for a future version and is not currently available to users. When activated, call logs (caller/callee username, channel name, call status) will be stored temporarily and audio/video streams will be processed in real time via LiveKit without being stored by ViviTap.

2.5 Subscription Data

  • Subscription status and plan (free, Family, Pro)
  • Purchase history managed by RevenueCat
  • An anonymized user identifier shared with RevenueCat for subscription management

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide core app functionality: sending messages, communication buttons, managing contacts
  • To deliver push notifications for incoming messages
  • To restore your preferences across sessions
  • To manage subscriptions and enforce plan limits
  • To monitor app stability and fix crashes
  • To enable account deletion and data erasure upon request

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or marketing purposes.

4. Legal Basis (GDPR Art. 6 & 9)

  • Art. 6(1)(b) — Performance of a contract: core app functionality
  • Art. 6(1)(a) — Consent: optional data such as profile photo
  • Art. 6(1)(f) — Legitimate interest: security, fraud prevention, app stability

Regarding health-related data: the use of a communication assistance application may implicitly reveal a communication disability. This constitutes special category data under GDPR Art. 9. We process this data solely to provide the app's core services, based on explicit consent given at registration (Art. 9(2)(a)). This explicit consent is collected through a dedicated consent screen at registration, where users must actively check three separate boxes: (1) acceptance of the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy, (2) acknowledgement that ViviTap may process data relating to their health or disability situation, and (3) confirmation of age eligibility. The consent record, including timestamp and version of the documents presented, is stored securely and associated with the user's account.

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 Firebase (Google LLC)

We use Firebase Authentication, Firestore, Firebase Storage, Firebase Cloud Messaging, and Firebase Crashlytics. User data (Firestore, Storage) is stored on servers located in europe-west3 (Frankfurt, Germany). Cloud Functions are deployed in europe-west1 (Belgium) and process data transiently without storing it. Crash reports may contain device type, OS version, and anonymized stack traces. More information: firebase.google.com/support/privacy

5.2 Google Sign-In (Google LLC)

If you choose to sign in with Google, your Google account email address and a Google-issued identifier are used solely for authentication purposes. More information: policies.google.com/privacy

5.3 LiveKit (LiveKit Inc.)

We use LiveKit for real-time audio and video calls. Audio and video streams are not recorded or stored by ViviTap. LiveKit may process connection metadata (IP address, connection timestamps) for the duration of the call. LiveKit applies Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) for EU data transfers. More information: livekit.io/privacy-policy

5.4 RevenueCat (RevenueCat Inc.)

We use RevenueCat to manage in-app subscriptions. RevenueCat processes your subscription status, purchase history, and an anonymized user identifier. Payments are handled by Google Play. RevenueCat applies SCCs for EU data transfers. More information: revenuecat.com/privacy

6. Data Retention

  • Account data: retained as long as the account is active
  • Messages: last 50 messages per contact; deleted when either user deletes their account
  • Call logs: not applicable currently (feature coming in a future version)
  • Push tokens: deleted upon account deletion
  • Crash reports: retained by Firebase Crashlytics for up to 90 days
  • Consent records: retained for the duration of the account plus 3 years after deletion (GDPR Art. 7)
  • Subscription history: retained by RevenueCat per their own retention policy and legal obligations
  • All data held by ViviTap: permanently deleted upon account deletion (Settings → Security → Delete Account)

7. Your Rights (GDPR)

You have the right to: access (Art. 15), rectification (Art. 16), erasure (Art. 17) via Settings → Security → Delete Account, restriction of processing (Art. 18), data portability (Art. 20), object to processing (Art. 21), and to withdraw consent at any time.

Contact us at contact@munyroth.com. We will respond within one month (Art. 12 GDPR).

You may also lodge a complaint with: LfDI Baden-Württemberg

8. International Data Transfers

Some third-party services (LiveKit, RevenueCat) may transfer data outside the EU. All such transfers are covered by Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission.

9. Minors

ViviTap is not a children's app, but it may be used by individuals under 16 years of age — including people with communication disabilities — under the supervision and with the explicit consent of a parent or legal guardian. In such cases, the parent or guardian creates and manages the account and is responsible for ensuring appropriate use.

By creating an account, you confirm that you are at least 16 years old, or that a parent or guardian has reviewed and accepted these terms and provided explicit consent on behalf of the minor.

10. Data Security

All data transmitted between the app and our servers is encrypted in transit using TLS/HTTPS. Firebase infrastructure provides encryption at rest. Access to user data is restricted to authenticated users via Firebase security rules and Cloud Functions.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes within the app. The date at the top of this page indicates when the policy was last revised.
