1. Introduction

ViviTap is a communication assistance application designed for people with communication disabilities and their caregivers ("helpers"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal data in compliance with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR / DSGVO) and applicable German law.

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Account Data

  • Email address (Firebase Authentication)
  • Username (chosen at registration)
  • Display name (optional)
  • Profile photo (optional, stored in Firebase Storage)
  • User role: helpee or helper

2.2 Communication Data

  • Messages sent and received between connected users (last 50 messages per contact, stored in Firestore)
  • Custom communication buttons (text, emoji, optional image)
  • Button usage statistics (frequency of use per button)

2.3 Technical Data

  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) push token for notifications
  • App preferences: language, grid layout, dark mode, audio volume, send method
  • Connection list (usernames of connected caregivers/users)
  • Crash reports and anonymized diagnostic data (Firebase Crashlytics)

2.4 Call Data

  • Call logs stored temporarily in Firestore: caller username, callee username, channel name, call status
  • Audio and video streams during calls (processed in real time via LiveKit, not stored by ViviTap)

2.5 Subscription Data

  • Subscription status and plan (free, Family, Pro)
  • Purchase history managed by RevenueCat
  • An anonymized user identifier shared with RevenueCat for subscription management

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide core app functionality: sending messages, making calls, managing contacts
  • To deliver push notifications for incoming messages and calls
  • To restore your preferences across sessions
  • To manage subscriptions and enforce plan limits
  • To monitor app stability and fix crashes
  • To enable account deletion and data erasure upon request

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or marketing purposes.

4. Legal Basis (GDPR Art. 6)

  • Art. 6(1)(b) — Performance of a contract: core app functionality
  • Art. 6(1)(a) — Consent: optional data such as profile photo
  • Art. 6(1)(f) — Legitimate interest: security, fraud prevention, app stability

Regarding health-related data: the use of a communication assistance application may implicitly reveal a communication disability. This constitutes special category data under GDPR Art. 9. We process this data solely to provide the app's core services, based on explicit consent given at registration (Art. 9(2)(a)).

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 Firebase (Google LLC)

We use Firebase Authentication, Firestore, Firebase Storage, Firebase Cloud Messaging, and Firebase Crashlytics. User data (Firestore, Storage) is stored on servers located in europe-west3 (Frankfurt, Germany). Cloud Functions are deployed in europe-west1 (Belgium) and process data transiently without storing it. Crash reports may contain device type, OS version, and anonymized stack traces. No personally identifiable information is intentionally included in crash reports. More information: firebase.google.com/support/privacy

5.2 Google Sign-In (Google LLC)

If you choose to sign in with Google, your Google account email address and a Google-issued identifier are used solely for authentication purposes. No additional Google profile data is accessed or stored. More information: policies.google.com/privacy

5.3 LiveKit (LiveKit Inc.)

We use LiveKit for real-time audio and video calls. Audio and video streams are transmitted through LiveKit's infrastructure and are not recorded or stored by ViviTap. LiveKit may process connection metadata (IP address, connection timestamps) for the duration of the call. LiveKit applies Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) for EU data transfers. More information: livekit.io/privacy-policy

5.4 RevenueCat (RevenueCat Inc.)

We use RevenueCat to manage in-app subscriptions. RevenueCat processes your subscription status, purchase history, and an anonymized user identifier. RevenueCat does not process payment card data directly — payments are handled by Google Play. RevenueCat applies Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) for EU data transfers. More information: revenuecat.com/privacy

6. Data Retention

  • Account data: retained as long as the account is active
  • Messages: last 50 messages per contact stored in Firestore; messages exchanged with a contact are permanently deleted when that contact deletes their account, or when you delete your own account
  • Call logs: deleted automatically within 24 hours
  • Push tokens: deleted upon account deletion
  • Crash reports: retained by Firebase Crashlytics for up to 90 days
  • Subscription history: retained by RevenueCat in accordance with their own retention policy and applicable legal obligations (including tax and accounting requirements)
  • All data held by ViviTap: permanently deleted upon account deletion (Settings → Security → Delete Account)

7. Your Rights (GDPR)

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data (Art. 15)
  • Rectification of inaccurate data (Art. 16)
  • Erasure — "right to be forgotten" (Art. 17) — available in Settings → Security → Delete Account
  • Restriction of processing (Art. 18)
  • Data portability (Art. 20) — contact us to request an export
  • Object to processing (Art. 21)
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise your rights, contact us at contact@munyroth.com. We will respond within one month of receiving your request (Art. 12 GDPR).

You may also lodge a complaint with: LfDI Baden-Württemberg

8. International Data Transfers

Some third-party services (LiveKit, RevenueCat) may transfer data outside the European Union. All such transfers are covered by Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission, ensuring an equivalent level of data protection.

9. Minors

Users under 16 years of age require verifiable parental or guardian consent. By creating an account, you confirm that you are at least 16 years old, or that a parent or guardian has provided consent.

10. Data Security

All data transmitted between the app and our servers is encrypted in transit using TLS/HTTPS. Firebase infrastructure provides encryption at rest. Access to user data is restricted to authenticated users via Firebase security rules and Cloud Functions.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes within the app. The date at the top of this page indicates when the policy was last revised.
