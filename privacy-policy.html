1. Introduction

ViviTap is a communication assistance application designed for people with communication disabilities and their caregivers ("helpers"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal data in compliance with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR / DSGVO) and applicable German law.

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Account Data

  • Email address (Firebase Authentication)
  • Username (chosen at registration)
  • Display name (optional)
  • Profile photo (optional, stored in Firebase Storage)
  • User role: helpee or helper

2.2 Communication Data

  • Messages sent and received between connected users (last 50 messages per contact, stored in Firestore)
  • Custom communication buttons (text, emoji, optional image)
  • Button usage statistics (frequency of use per button)

2.3 Technical Data

  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) push token for notifications
  • App preferences: language, grid layout, dark mode, audio volume, send method
  • Connection list (usernames of connected caregivers/users)

2.4 Call Data

  • Call logs stored temporarily in Firestore: caller username, callee username, channel name, call status
  • Audio and video streams during calls (processed in real time via Agora, not stored by ViviTap)

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide core app functionality: sending messages, making calls, managing contacts
  • To deliver push notifications for incoming messages and calls
  • To restore your preferences across sessions
  • To enable account deletion and data erasure upon request

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or marketing purposes.

4. Legal Basis (GDPR Art. 6)

  • Art. 6(1)(b) — Performance of a contract: core app functionality
  • Art. 6(1)(a) — Consent: optional data such as profile photo
  • Art. 6(1)(f) — Legitimate interest: security, fraud prevention, app stability

Regarding health-related data: the use of a communication assistance application may implicitly reveal a communication disability. This constitutes special category data under GDPR Art. 9. We process this data solely to provide the app's core services, based on explicit consent given at registration (Art. 9(2)(a)).

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 Firebase (Google LLC)

We use Firebase Authentication, Firestore, Firebase Storage, and Firebase Cloud Messaging. All data is stored on servers located in europe-west3 (Frankfurt, Germany). More information: firebase.google.com/support/privacy

5.2 Agora (Agora.io Inc.)

We use Agora for real-time audio and video calls. Audio and video streams are transmitted through Agora's infrastructure, which may include servers outside the European Union. This transfer is necessary for the performance of the service. Agora applies Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs). More information: agora.io/en/privacy-policy

6. Data Retention

  • Account data: retained as long as the account is active
  • Messages: last 50 messages per contact stored in Firestore
  • Call logs: deleted automatically after the call ends or within 24 hours
  • Push tokens: deleted upon account deletion
  • All data: permanently deleted upon account deletion (Settings → Delete Account)

7. Your Rights (GDPR)

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data (Art. 15)
  • Rectification of inaccurate data (Art. 16)
  • Erasure — "right to be forgotten" (Art. 17) — available in Settings → Delete Account
  • Restriction of processing (Art. 18)
  • Data portability (Art. 20) — contact us to request an export
  • Object to processing (Art. 21)
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise your rights: contact@munyroth.com

You may also lodge a complaint with: LfDI Baden-Württemberg

8. Minors

Users under 16 years of age require verifiable parental or guardian consent. By creating an account, you confirm that you are at least 16 years old, or that a parent or guardian has provided consent.

9. Data Security

All data transmitted between the app and our servers is encrypted in transit using TLS/HTTPS. Firebase infrastructure provides encryption at rest.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes within the app.
